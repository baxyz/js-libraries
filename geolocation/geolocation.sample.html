distance()

Parameters:

  • in WGS'84 (GPS)

    Center latitude

    The latitude coordinate of the center of the targeted area
  • in WGS'84 (GPS)

    Center longitude

    The longitude coordinate of the center of the targeted area
  • in WGS'84 (GPS)

    Point latitude

    The latitude coordinate of the requested point
  • in WGS'84 (GPS)

    Point longitude

    The longitude coordinate of the requested point

Sample:

Input:

Geolocation.distance(53.3381985, -6.2592576, 52.986375, -6.043701);

Output (expected):

41.6768391
